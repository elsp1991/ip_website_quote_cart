Overview

Website Online Quotation System adds a dedicated quotation cart to your Odoo eCommerce store, allowing customers to request quotes for products instead of purchasing directly. Ideal for B2B businesses that don't want to share their prices publicly, or for products that require custom pricing and negotiation.

‚ú® Why Use an Online Quotation System?

Benefit from the self-service convenience of online shopping in a B2B context where prices aren't public. Your customers can browse your catalog, select products with variants and options, and submit quote requests - all without seeing prices. Works seamlessly alongside the regular shopping cart for products with public pricing.

How It Works

  1. Add to Quote - Customers click the "Add to Quote" button on products
  2. Configure Options - Select variants and optional products if available
  3. Review Quotation Cart - View and modify items in the dedicated quote cart
  4. Describe Application - An extra step where customers can describe their use case or requirements
  5. Submit Request - Customer submits the quote request with their details

The module works immediately after installation - no enable step required. Configure options in Website ‚Üí Configuration ‚Üí Settings:

  • Quote Cart Visibility - Control whether the quotation cart icon is persistent or hidden in the header
  • Quick Add Button - Enable or disable the quick "Add to Quote" button on product thumbnails
