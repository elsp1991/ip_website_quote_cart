Quote Cart For Quotation Request


The "Quote Cart For Quotation Request" is designed to enhance the shopping experience on the website by offering customers a seamless and transparent process for obtaining pricing information and making adjustments for several products at a time.

Documentation

Navigate to Settings > Website > eCommerce , and then activate the Quote Button. The Quote button is used to enable or disable the quotation functionality .



User can see the Quote Cart button has been added.



When the user clicks on the product, the "Add to Quote" button will appear along with the "Add to Cart" button.



If the Products price is zero ,Â then only "Add to Quote" button will be appear.



In Products list, the add to quote button will appear beside the add to cart button.



On clicking the "Add to quote" button, that product will be added to the Quote Cart.



On clicking the "Process Quote" button, address page will be opened.



Clicking on the "Submit Quote" button, it will create a new quotation.



Users can see their requested quotations in their "My Account" section by clicking on the "Requested Quotations" button.



The created quotation is visible in the Sales module.



Clicking on the "Add to quote" button will also display a popup notification.



In My Account > Quotations to review section, the expired quotations will be removed after 10 days.
